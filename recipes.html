Ramen

Ingredients

Ramen noodles
Garlic and ginger
Broth (chicken)
Toppings like some panko, egg, chili oil, etc.

Instructions

Stir-fry the garlic and ginger.
Add your broth.
Add and cook your noodles in the broth(optional: add scallions for more flavor)
Top it off with some of your favorite toppings.

Udon

Ingredients

Udon noodles
Soy and dark mushroom soy sauce
Rice Vinegar
Liquid sweeteners
Sesame oil and toasted sesame seeds

Instructions

Cook Udon noodles for 3-4 minutes.
Add in the rest of the ingredients.
Mix and enjoy!
