Dango

Ingredients

Glutinous rice flour
Glutinous sweet rice flour
Powdered sugar
Matcha powder
Pink food coloring

Instructions

Soak skewers in water.
Mix the rice flour and hot water. Use your hands to mold the dough when it comes together.
Add matcha powder or food coloring for flavor and color.
Shape dango into little balls and boil the dango balls until they float to the top. Boil for 2 more minutes after it floats to the top.
Cool and skewer the dango.

Taiyaki

Ingredients

Butter
Light brown sugar
Eggs
Cake flour
Baking powder
Warm milk
Honey
Vanilla extract
Light soy sauce

Instructions

Mix the butter with suger, add the eggs one at a time.
Add HALF of the flour, then all of the milk, and the rest of the flour.
Pour the batter into a fish shaped mold or what shape you desire.
Add red bean paste(optional) and enjoy!
