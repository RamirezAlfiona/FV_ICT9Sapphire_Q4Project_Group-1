Katsudon

Ingredients

Boneless pork loin or pork chop
All Purpose Flour
Egg
Salt and pepper
Panko breadcrumbs

Instructions

Coat the pork in flour, then coat in the egg wash, and then coat it in the breadcrumbs.
Heat the oil to 340 degrees to cook the pork until golden.
Cut the Tonkatsu with 1 inch slices.
Serve on top of hot rice and enjoy!

Omurice

Ingredients

Mushroom
Onion and green onion
Carrot
Spam
Cooked rice
Soy sauce
Ketchup
Eggs
Milk

Instructions

Heat oil and add green onions and onion. When fragrant, add carrot, spam, and mushroom.
Add in the rice, soy sauce, ketchup and mix. Shape into a bowl
Mix eggs, milk, and salt. Pour egg mixture in the pan. Scramble the eggs until 80% of it is cooked.
Place the omelette on top of the rice. Add some ketchup on top. And enjoy!
