Nigiri

Ingredients

Sushi fish
Sushi vinegar rice

Instructions

Wet your hands to shape the sushi rice.
Place the sushi fish on top of the rice. (Make sure to wash your hands between each fish you place)
Serve with soy sauce and enjoy!

Maki

Ingredients

Nori
Sushi rice
Fillings (Avocado, raw seafood, imitation crab, mango, etc.)
Liquid sweeteners

Instructions

Place your nori down on the bamboo rolling mat with the shiny side down.
Cover nori with rice, leaving some space at the end.
Place your chosen fillings in a line 1/3 of the way up the rice. Roll.
Cut into small pieces and serve with soy sauce.
